# TinniMate — Marketing Overview & Launch Strategy

**Product:** TinniMate (ứng dụng trị liệu ù tai)
**Market:** Vietnam (Vietnamese-speaking)
**Launch Window:** 90 days (Q2 2026)
**Budget:** <$500/month (organic-heavy)

---

## Executive Summary

TinniMate is positioned as Vietnam's first science-backed tinnitus management app. We own the Vietnamese market (zero competitors) with proprietary tech (Zentones, Notch Therapy) + clinical depth (5 WHO questionnaires).

**Win Strategy:**
1. **Quick launch** (App Store/Play Store approval)
2. **Word-of-mouth** + early audiologist partnerships for credibility
3. **Organic social** (Facebook, TikTok, YouTube) to build awareness
4. **SEO** for long-term symptom-based traffic
5. **Low CPA** (<$2 target) via strategic paid ads on high-intent keywords

**Target:** 5K–10K Free signups + 500–1.5K Premium conversions by Day 90.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Vietnam Tinnitus Market
- **Prevalence:** 8–12M Vietnamese adults (15–20% of population) experience tinnitus
- **Severity:** 2–3M suffer severe tinnitus affecting quality of life
- **Awareness Gap:** 60–70% don't know tinnitus is treatable. Many resign to medication or live with it.
- **Healthcare Gap:** Limited audiologists in rural Vietnam. Urban clinics charge ₫1–5M for consultations. No accessible, affordable solution.

### Economic Opportunity
- **Digital Health Adoption:** Vietnamese healthcare spending increasing 8–12% annually
- **Mobile-First:** 97% of Vietnamese internet users on mobile (low PC penetration)
- **Subscription Comfort:** Vietnamese accept SaaS subscriptions ($4.99/month = ~1–2 cups of coffee)
- **Willingness to Pay:** Severe tinnitus sufferers spend ₫100K–1M+ monthly on remedies (medication, audiologist visits)

### Window of Opportunity
- **12–24 months** before global apps (MindEar, Oto) localize to Vietnamese
- **Now:** Establish market dominance + brand loyalty before competition arrives
- **Future:** Build network effects (audiologist partnerships, clinical trials, B2B licensing)

## Channel Strategy

### Channel Priority & Timeline

#### Channel 1: Facebook Ads (Tier 1 — Immediate ROI)
**Why:** 77% of Vietnamese internet users on Facebook. High intent audience (symptom + sleep + anxiety keywords).

**Audience Targeting:**
- Age: 25–55
- Interests: Health + sleep + anxiety + tinnitus keywords (if available)
- Lookalike audiences from landing page visitors
- Retargeting: Website visitors who didn't convert

**Ad Formats:**
1. **Awareness Video (15–30 sec):** "Why you hear ringing (and what to do about it)"
   - Shows vicious cycle (tinnitus → anxiety → sleep loss → worse tinnitus)
   - Ends with CTA: "Try free for 5 messages"

2. **Testimonial Video (30–45 sec):** User success story
   - Before/after sleep quality
   - Simple editing, authentic voice
   - 2–3 testimonials rotated weekly

3. **Lead Magnet Ad:** "Free tinnitus assessment" (THI quiz)
   - Landing page collects email
   - Email sequence with app download + onboarding tips

**Budget & Timeline:**
- Week 1–2: $100 (testing audiences, creative, landing page)
- Week 3–4: $150/week (scaling winning ads)
- Month 2+: $200/week (sustained acquisition)

**Target Metrics:**
- CPC: <₫10K ($0.40)
- CTR: ≥3%
- Landing page conversion (visitor → signup): ≥15%
- Overall CPA: <₫50K ($2)

**Milestones:**
- Week 1: 100 signups from ads
- Week 4: 500–1K signups from ads
- Month 3: 2K–3K signups from ads

**Hashtags:**
- #TinnitusTreatment, #ÙTai, #SoundTherapy, #SleepHelp, #MentalHealth (Vietnamese trending)

**Budget & Timeline:**
- Week 1–2: Organic only (build audience)
- Week 3–4: $50 TikTok ads (top-performing videos)

## Messaging & Creative Direction

### Brand Tone
- **Empathetic:** Acknowledge suffering. "Ù tai ảnh hưởng đến cuộc sống của bạn. Chúng tôi hiểu."
- **Hopeful:** Science-backed solutions exist. "Có cách để cải thiện. Bạn không cô đơn."
- **Clear:** No jargon. Explain neuroscience simply.
- **Action-Oriented:** "Hãy bắt đầu ngay hôm nay." (Start today.)

### Campaign Themes

#### Month 1: Awareness ("You're Not Alone")
**Message:** Normalize tinnitus. Introduce problem + solution.
**Visual:** Calm, soothing colors (blues, greens). People sleeping peacefully.
**Ad Copy:** "15-20% of people experience tinnitus. TinniMate helps you sleep peacefully again. Free for 5 messages."

#### Month 2: Solution ("Try Free First")
**Message:** Lower friction. Show product value.
**Visual:** App screenshots, feature demos, testimonial clips.
**Ad Copy:** "Try TinniMate free. Get 5 AI messages, 3 therapy sounds, and a hearing test. No credit card needed."

#### Month 3: Conversion ("Join Thousands")
**Message:** Social proof. Urgency (launch offer expires).
**Visual:** User testimonials, ratings, growth numbers.
**Ad Copy:** "500+ users sleep better with TinniMate. Join today. Premium only ₫99K/month."
