# TinniMate — Project Overview & PDR

**Product:** TinniMate (ứng dụng trị liệu ù tai)
**Current Stage:** Pre-launch (App Store/Play Store submission pending)
**Target Market:** Vietnam
**Primary Objective:** Deliver science-backed tinnitus therapy with proprietary tech + clinical depth

---

## Executive Summary

TinniMate is Vietnam's first comprehensive tinnitus management app. We combine **proprietary audio technology (Zentones, Notch Therapy)** with **evidence-based clinical tools (5 WHO-recognized questionnaires, TRT, CBT-i)** to address 8-12M Vietnamese adults suffering from tinnitus with no localized solution.

**Why Now:** Zero Vietnamese-first tinnitus apps exist. Global competitors (MindEar, Oto, AudioCardio) lack Vietnamese language + clinical depth. Market demand is acute—tinnitus causes sleep disruption, anxiety, reduced productivity across Vietnam's urban workforce.

---

## Product Description

### Core Technology
- **Zentones:** Proprietary fractal melody system. Fractal patterns (self-similar across scales) match the auditory cortex's hierarchical processing, promoting faster habituation. Exclusive to TinniMate.
- **Notch Therapy:** Precise frequency filter targeting tinnitus frequency. User completes audiometry → Notch Therapy suppresses that frequency at safe levels. Backed by Meikle et al. (2020).
- **Sound Mixer:** 11 therapy sounds (white/pink/brown noise, nature sounds, healing frequencies). Customizable layering + timer control.
- **Audiometry:** Hughson-Westlake method (6 frequencies: 250Hz, 500Hz, 1kHz, 2kHz, 4kHz, 8kHz). Free users: 1 test/month. Premium users: unlimited.

### Clinical Tools (Evidence-Based)
1. **THI** (Tinnitus Handicap Inventory) — 25-item, measures handicap level (mild/moderate/severe)
2. **TFI** (Tinnitus Functional Index) — 27-item, functional impact across 8 domains
3. **PHQ-9** (Patient Health Questionnaire) — 9-item depression screening
4. **GAD-7** (Generalized Anxiety Disorder) — 7-item anxiety screening
5. **ISI** (Insomnia Severity Index) — 7-item sleep disturbance assessment

### Tinni AI Chatbot
- **24/7 Vietnamese + English support** (bilingual)
- Empathetic, non-judgmental tone. Speaks to emotional suffering + scientific solutions.
- Guides symptom tracking, suggests tools, administers questionnaires, explains tinnitus neuroscience.
- Safety protocols: No diagnosis/prescription. Crisis resources (Vietnam: 1800 599 920).

### Therapy Methodologies
- **TRT** (Tinnitus Retraining Therapy, Jastreboff method): Combine sound + counseling to retrain the auditory system.
- **CBT-i** (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia): Sleep hygiene + thought reframing for tinnitus-related insomnia.
- **Habituation Focus:** Train brain to ignore tinnitus through consistent sound therapy + psychological support.

## Target Audience Personas

### Persona 1: "Linh" — Light Sufferer, Sleep-Focused
- **Age:** 28–40, urban professional (Hanoi/HCMC)
- **Income:** $500–1,500/month
- **Tinnitus:** Mild ringing, manageable daytime. Worsens at night when quiet.
- **Pain Point:** Insomnia caused by tinnitus. Can't enjoy silence anymore. Seeks quick fixes, skeptical of medication.
- **Motivation:** Restore sleep quality. Understand why this is happening (psychoeducation).
- **Conversion Path:** Free→Premium (after 2–3 weeks of sound therapy + positive sleep impact).

### Persona 2: "Anh Minh" — Severe Case, Medical-Seeking
- **Age:** 45–60, business owner/employee
- **Income:** $1,000–3,000/month
- **Tinnitus:** Loud roaring/hissing. 24/7. Severe impact on concentration, mood.
- **Pain Point:** Tried audiologists (expensive, limited solutions). Tired of being told "no cure." Desperate for relief.
- **Motivation:** Clinical validation. Structured treatment plan. Track progress over months.
- **Conversion Path:** Free→Pro (needs audiologist consultation, detailed assessments, long-term commitment).

### Persona 3: "Chị Hương" — Family Caregiver
- **Age:** 35–50, often caring for elderly parent with tinnitus
- **Income:** $800–2,000/month
- **Tinnitus:** Parent's tinnitus. Affects family dynamic (parent irritable, withdrawn).
- **Pain Point:** Limited healthcare access in rural areas. Parent skeptical of apps. Need multi-user support.
- **Motivation:** Help family member. Save on medical costs. Easy-to-use interface for older adults.
- **Conversion Path:** Free→Pro (family sharing feature to manage 3 accounts).

**Total TAM:** 8–12M Vietnamese with tinnitus. **SAM:** 2–3M urban adults (Hanoi, HCMC, Da Nang) able to afford app. **SOM (Year 1):** 10K–50K active users.
