# TinniMate — Product Roadmap (90-Day Launch + 12-Month Vision)

**Timeline:** Q2 2026 (90-day launch) → Q2 2027 (12-month vision)
**Primary Goal:** Launch app. Acquire 5K–10K Free users. Drive 500–1.5K Premium conversions by Day 90.
**Secondary Goals:** Build audiologist partnerships. Establish brand presence. Validate market fit.

---

## 90-Day Launch Phase

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days 1–30)
**Objective:** Prepare for launch. Build foundation. Gather credibility.

#### Key Milestones
| Milestone | Target Date | Success Criteria |
|-----------|-----------|------------------|
| App submitted (App Store + Play Store) | Day 5 | Submission confirmed, no blockers |
| Landing page live | Day 10 | Mobile-responsive, ≥90 Lighthouse score |
| Social accounts created + seeded | Day 10 | 50+ followers each (Facebook, TikTok, YouTube) |
| Expert testimonials collected | Day 20 | 3–5 written/video testimonials from audiologists |
| Email list seeded | Day 25 | 500 emails collected from landing page |
| App approved | Day 30 | Live on both stores (target 90% confidence) |

#### Activities & Owners
**Product Team:**
- Final bug fixes + optimization for app launch
- Set up analytics (GA4, Mixpanel, Firebase) for tracking
- Prepare in-app messaging (onboarding flow, push notifications)
- Create user account recovery + support systems

**Marketing Team:**
- Create landing page (Figma → Webflow or Vercel)
- Design hero video (explainer animation + product demo)
- Collect audiologist testimonials (outreach + filming)
- Set up email platform (Mailchimp, Substack, or equivalent)
- Create content calendar for 90 days
- Prepare paid ad accounts (Facebook, TikTok, YouTube)
- Create 5–10 organic social media posts

**Design Team:**
- Finalize app UI polish (onboarding, chat, questionnaires)
- Create social media templates (Facebook, TikTok, YouTube)
- Design ad creative (video, carousel, static)
- Create testimonial graphics

**Operations:**
- Secure payment processing (Stripe, ZaloPay, Momo)
- Set up customer support system (email, in-app chat)
- Create FAQ + knowledge base
- Plan crisis response (bugs, negative reviews)

#### Budget
- App Store + Play Store fees: $100

- Email platform: $20
- Design assets: $50 (if contractor-based)

## 12-Month Vision (Post-Launch)

### Q3 2026 (Months 4–6): Scale & Optimize
**Objective:** Scale acquisition to 50K Free users. Drive Pro tier adoption. Build B2B pipeline.

#### Focus Areas
1. **Retention Optimization**
   - Implement engagement loops (daily streaks, level system, badges)
   - Personalized therapy recommendations (based on symptom severity, preferences)
   - Retention campaigns (push notifications, email re-engagement)
   - Target: 40%+ 30-day retention → 50%+ by end of Q3

2. **Revenue Diversification**
   - Launch Pro tier with audiologist consultations (live)
   - Launch family sharing feature (Pro: 3 accounts)
   - Explore B2B licensing (clinics, hospitals)
   - Target MRR: ₫3–5M ($120–200) by end of Q3

3. **Audiologist Network Expansion**
   - 5–10 partnerships signed (consultation referral revenue share)
   - Co-marketing campaigns with partner clinics
   - Create "TinniMate-endorsed audiologist" badge
   - Target: 50+ referring audiologists by end of Q3

4. **Content & SEO**
   - Publish 20+ blog posts (SEO targeting high-intent keywords)
   - 10+ YouTube videos (explainers, testimonials)
   - Target: 1K+ organic visits/month by end of Q3

#### Milestones
- Day 120: 25K Free signups (cumulative)
- Day 150: 5K Premium users, ₫1.5M MRR
- Day 180: 50K Free signups, 10K Premium users, ₫3–5M MRR

#### Budget
- Paid ads: $1.5K/month ($4.5K total)
- Content creation: $200/month ($600 total)
- Partnerships: $100/month ($300 total, mostly communication)
- **Total: ~$5.4K (Average: $1.8K/month)**

### Q4 2026 (Months 7–9): Expansion & Clinical Validation
**Objective:** Clinical study. International expansion (initial). Profitability path.

#### Focus Areas
1. **Clinical Validation**
   - Partner with Vietnamese university (audio/audiology program)
   - Conduct RCT comparing TinniMate vs usual care
   - Publish results in peer-reviewed journal
   - Prepare FDA/CE mark application (if needed for international)

2. **International Expansion (Soft Launch)**
   - Add English fully (currently bilingual)
   - Soft launch in 1–2 English-speaking countries (Philippines, Cambodia)
   - Test market fit + payment infrastructure
   - Target: 5K–10K international signups by end of Q4

3. **Product Maturity**
   - AI chatbot (Tinni) improvements (GPT-4, multilingual)
   - Offline mode (cache sounds, questionnaires, transcripts)
   - Apple Health + Google Fit integration
   - Wearable integration (sleep tracking from smartwatch)

4. **Business Model Expansion**
   - B2B licensing: 2–3 clinic pilots (revenue share)
   - Clinical trial recruitment: Partner with pharma companies ($10K–50K per trial)
   - API monetization: Open API for health platforms (partner integrations)

#### Milestones
- Day 210: 100K Free signups (cumulative, Vietnam)
- Day 240: RCT published (or under review)
- Day 270: 15K Premium users (Vietnam), 2K international signups

#### Budget
- Paid ads (Vietnam + international): $2K/month ($6K total)
- Clinical study: $2K–3K (university partnership, modest)
- Content/PR: $200/month ($600 total)
- **Total: ~$9.6K (Average: $3.2K/month)**

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Key Risks
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Competitor Entry** (Global apps localize to Vietnamese) | High | Establish market dominance in 12 months. Build brand loyalty. Differentiate on local support + partnerships. |
| **App Store Approval Delays** | High | Submit early (Day 5). Prepare documentation. Have backup web version ready. |
| **Payment Processing Issues** | Medium | Test ZaloPay + Momo early. Have backup payment method (Stripe). Monitor transaction success rate. |
| **User Retention Low** | High | Optimize onboarding. Implement daily streaks, personalization. A/B test notifications. |
| **Negative Reviews** | Medium | Respond to all reviews within 24 hours. Address issues quickly. Encourage satisfied users to rate. |
| **Clinical Credibility Gap** | Medium | Partner with audiologists early. Publish clinical data. Get media coverage. |
| **Regulatory Uncertainty** | Low | Monitor Vietnamese healthcare regulations. Ensure PDPA compliance. Consult legal early. |

## Quarterly Checkpoints & Adjustments

### Checkpoint Process
Every quarter (Month 3, 6, 9, 12):
1. **Review KPIs** against targets
2. **Analyze what worked** (high CAC channels, high-retention cohorts, top features)
3. **Identify gaps** (what underperformed? Why?)
4. **Adjust roadmap** (double down on winners, sunset losers, pivot if needed)
5. **Update this document** (reflect new learnings)

### Decision Thresholds
- **If 30-day retention <35%:** Pause paid ads. Focus on product retention mechanics.
- **If CPA >$3:** Shift budget to organic/referral. Pause underperforming channels.
- **If app store rating <3.8:** Focus on bug fixes, user support. Pause marketing until resolved.
- **If MRR not on pace:** Optimize conversion funnel, extend free trial, run promotion.
