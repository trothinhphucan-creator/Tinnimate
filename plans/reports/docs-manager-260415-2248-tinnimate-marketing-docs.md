## Key Decisions Made

### 1. Market Positioning
- **Primary:** Vietnam's first science-backed tinnitus app (zero competitors)
- **Secondary:** Proprietary tech (Zentones, Notch Therapy) + clinical depth (5 WHO questionnaires)
- **Price:** $4.99/month Premium (50% cheaper than global competitors)
- **Tone:** Empathetic + hopeful + informed (not clinical/cold or dismissive)

### 2. Acquisition Strategy
- **Primary channel:** Facebook Ads (77% Vietnamese penetration, high intent audience)
- **Secondary:** TikTok (growing, symptom awareness), YouTube (authority), Zalo (community)
- **Budget:** <$500/month sustained, front-loaded in launch phase ($200→$350→$450 over 90 days)
- **CPA target:** <$2 (achievable at 10% conversion rate with $4.99 LTV)

### 3. Go-to-Market Timeline
- **Phase 1 (30 days):** Setup + credibility (app submission, landing page, testimonials)
- **Phase 2 (30 days):** Launch visibility + early acquisition (paid ads, organic social, PR)
- **Phase 3 (30 days):** Optimize + scale (refine messaging, expand channels, partnerships)
- **Year 1:** Scale (50K→100K→200K signups), profitability (MRR break-even)

### 4. Content Strategy
- **80/20 split:** 80% content owned (blog, YouTube, email), 20% paid ads (Facebook, TikTok)
- **Pillars:** Education (build trust), Solution (reduce friction), Science (credibility), Community (retention)
- **SEO focus:** Vietnamese symptom keywords (low competition, high intent)
- **Testimonial-heavy:** Video case studies (authentic, emotional impact)

### 5. Revenue Assumptions
- **Year 1 MRR:** ₫8–12M ($300–500) by Month 12
- **Premium penetration:** 10–15% of Free users (500–1.5K by Day 90)
- **LTV:CAC ratio:** Target 4:1 (LTV ~$120 over 1 year, CAC ~$30)
- **Future:** B2B licensing (clinics), clinical trials (pharma), audiologist marketplace (revenue share)
