# TinniMate Marketing Research Report
**Date:** 2026-04-15 | **Status:** MARKETING INITIALIZATION ANALYSIS

---

## 1. Product Description

**TinniMate** — AI-powered tinnitus therapy platform combining clinical evidence-based treatments (TRT, CBT-i, Notch Therapy) with personalized sound therapy and AI companion chatbot (Tinni 💙). Positioned as "#1 Tinnitus Therapy App" helping users manage tinnitus through daily sound therapy, hearing assessments, progress tracking, and stress relief.

---

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary Demographics:**
- Age: 25-70+ (peak: 40-65 with age-related hearing loss)
- Geography: Vietnam-first (bilingual support: VI/EN)
- Tech comfort: Mid to high (mobile-first, some desktop users)
- Income: Mid to upper-middle (premium tier: 99K-199K VND/month ≈ $4-8/month)

**Psychographics:**
- Tinnitus sufferers (chronic, diagnosed)
- Health-conscious, self-improvement motivated
- Seek evidence-based solutions, not "magic cures"
- Often experiencing sleep disruption, anxiety, stress
- Want to understand root cause, not just symptom relief
- Doctor-recommended seekers (ENT specialist endorsement present)

**Use Cases:**
- Nighttime sleep assistance (sound masking)
- Daily stress/anxiety management
- Progress tracking with clinical metrics (THI, TFI scores)
- Medical professionals using for patient monitoring

---

## 3. Key Features & USPs

**Exclusive/Premium Features:**
- **Zentones** — Proprietary fractal melody sound technology (personalized to tinnitus frequency)
- **Notch Therapy** — Precise frequency filtering of tinnitus frequency (neural inhibition)
- **AI Companion (Tinni)** — 24/7 multilingual chatbot with 6 specialized tools:
  - Hearing Test (6-frequency audiometry: Hughson-Westlake standard)
  - Sound Therapy (11+ curated sounds, Sound Mixer: up to 4 layers)
  - Clinical Assessment (THI, TFI, ISI, PHQ-9, GAD-7 scales)
  - Relaxation Tools (CBT-i, breathing exercises: 4-7-8 technique)
  - Daily Check-in (mood, sleep, tinnitus loudness tracking)
  - Symptom Analysis/Consultation

**Clinical Methods Featured:**
- TRT (Tinnitus Retraining Therapy) — Jastreboff method
- CBT-i (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia) — WHO standard
- Audiometry & Hearing Assessment — Clinical gold standard

**Supporting Tools:**
- Daily journal & habit tracking
- Sleep Mode (optimized therapy)
- Progress visualizations (charts, metrics)
- Multi-user family sharing (Pro tier)
- API access for healthcare providers (Pro tier)

## 7. Market Positioning & Competitive Angle

**Positioning:**
- "#1 Tinnitus Therapy App" (Vietnam-first claim, expanding)
- Science-backed (TRT, CBT-i, clinical scales)
- Accessible (freemium, multilingual, mobile-first)
- Human + AI hybrid (Tinni chatbot as "companion," not replacement)

**Differentiation:**
- **Proprietary tech:** Zentones (fractal sound), Notch Therapy
- **Clinical rigor:** Uses WHO-recognized scales (THI, TFI, PHQ-9, GAD-7)
- **AI + human:** 24/7 Tinni + optional expert consultation (Pro)
- **Holistic approach:** Not just sound masking; addresses anxiety, sleep, lifestyle

**Implied Competitors:**
- Other tinnitus apps (Widex Zen, ReSound, etc.) — lacks personalized AI
- General wellness apps (meditation, sleep apps) — lacks clinical focus
- Traditional ENT-only care — expensive, limited access
